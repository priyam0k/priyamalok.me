I am an aspiring P&C actuarial model developer specializing at the intersection of actuarial science, data engineering, and machine learning.

I build the data backbone for modern analytics using Python, SQL, and cloud platforms, focusing on robust data pipelines. I leverage predictive analytics and machine learning for underwriting and claims, embracing the 'actuarial engineer' mindset with a focus on transparent AI.

Ultimately, I translate complex analysis into actionable business insights through effective data storytelling and visualization, connecting technical work to strategic business value.
